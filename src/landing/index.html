Tabflow is an AI-powered extension that organizes tabs into dedicated workspaces, schedules folders to open automatically, secures private content with AES-256 encryption, and lets your personal AI assistant control your browser for you.

Download .zip Installation Guide

AI Assistant

Command your browser effortlessly. Simply type Close my YouTube tabs, Group my research, or Open my Work folder and let AI do the rest.

Secure Folders

Lock your private workspaces with AES-256 encryption. Not even the AI can read your locked tabs until you enter your password.

Smart Scheduling

Set a timer to automatically open your Morning Routine folder at 9 AM, and auto-close social media tabs after 2 hours.

Smart Launcher

Hit Cmd/Ctrl + K to instantly open the launcher. You can type AI commands or use the search bar to access and manage all your saved workspaces.

Quick Access Popup

Click the extension icon to view up to 5 favorite pinned folders. Instantly add tabs, open, close, or lock your most important folders with a single click.

How to Install

1

Download the Zip

Click the download button above and extract the downloaded tabflow_extension.zip file into a folder on your computer.

2

Enable Developer Mode

Open your browser and navigate to the extensions page:
• chrome://extensions (Chrome)
• edge://extensions (Edge)
• brave://extensions (Brave)
Then toggle "Developer mode" ON.

3

Load Unpacked

Click the "Load unpacked" button in the top left corner and select the folder you extracted in Step 1. The extension is now installed!

4

Allow in Incognito (Optional)

To use Tabflow in private tabs, click "Details" on the extension card and toggle ON "Allow in Incognito".
